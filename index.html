GhostNordicCameras is an art project that turns Norway’s traffic and weather cameras into a thrilling adventure. As you travel through the country, you can capture eerie, one-of-a-kind photos in remote and breathtaking locations. These unique images, which can’t be taken any other way, are great for sharing on social media. The project encourages you to explore Norway’s landscapes, turning your travels into a game of discovering and collecting spooky selfies in some of the country's most hidden, though very beautiful, spots.
